Project details

BovEye turns multispectral drone imagery into a cattle-detection workflow for aerial livestock monitoring. The work centered on preserving RGBN signal, adapting the detector, and making evaluation easy to inspect.

Problem

Detect small cattle from aerial imagery with changing altitude, vegetation, and multispectral channels that regular RGB detectors do not expect.

Build

Prepared RGBN inputs, adapted the RF-DETR patch embedding path, and kept labels, splits, and transforms aligned through training.

Evaluation

Created threshold sweeps and readable summaries so operating points could be selected with evidence instead of guesswork.

Stack

PyTorch RF-DETR RGBN detection evaluation
